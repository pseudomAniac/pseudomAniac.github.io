  • About
    T-Rocks Construction Limited is formed to target rural road works in PNG.

  • Mission
    To support transport infrastructure for the rural population of Papua New Guinea by ensuring that economic growth is achieved.

  • Description
    This company is established to carry out rural road maintenance in Papua New Guinea. Its aim is to build affordable road in rural areas to enhance Papua New Guinea transport infrastructure so that it can enable economic growth.

We are part of the Tirokave Holdings Corporation, a company for the rural people.

We are all about the rural communities and the people. Below are four of our sister companies/organisations
